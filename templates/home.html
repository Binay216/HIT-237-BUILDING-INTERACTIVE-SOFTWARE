Northern Territory Remote Housing Repairs

Empowering tenants in remote communities to report, track, and resolve housing issues.

The NT has a public housing waitlist of almost 6,000 people and a homelessness rate 12 times the national average. A $4 billion, 10-year housing agreement aims to build 2,700 homes across 73 communities and 27 town camps. This system helps tenants log essential repairs — from broken air conditioning to plumbing and structural damage — and track them through to completion.
